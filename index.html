How to Play

The goal is to sort the films into groups of four.

Rules:

  • Each group has a unique theme or connection.
  • You get a four mistakes. Pay attention to the "Mistakes remaining" counter.
  • Select four items and click "Submit" to see if you identified a group.
  • The "Shuffle" button rearranges the items on the grid.
  • The "Deselect All" button clears your current selections.

Tips:

  • Examples of some groups might be Genre, Featuring a certain actor, Director, Theme, Topic etc
  • Watch out for misdirects!
